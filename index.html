Overall Suicide Rate per 100k population across the world

Female or Male?

When comparing the overall suicide committed for male and female it can be seen that male are more likely to commit suicide comapre to female.

Top 10 Countries with Highest Suicide Rate per Age Group

Lithuania has the highest suicide rate per its population with majority of rate coming from 55-74 age group & 35-54 age group with a suicide rate of 2496 and 2696 respectively. Moreover, Korea has the hgihest suicide rate for 75+ age group with a suicide rate of 4294 per its population.

Average Suicide Rates per 100k population across different Age Groups

There is a spike increase in suicide rate for all age group between year 1994-1996 except for 5-14 years age group. Upon further exploration, elderly age group of 75+ years has the highest suicide rate reaching 4709/100K population in 1995. On the other hand, younger age group such as those from 5-14 years old are least worrying showing non fluctuating trend acorss the years.
